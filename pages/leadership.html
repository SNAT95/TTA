Samuel Nii Ayi Tettey

Founder, CEO & Board Chairman

Samuel Nii Ayi Tettey is a Ghana-based Information Technology professional with over 8 years of progressive experience in IT operations, business applications, systems administration, cybersecurity, technical support, and digital transformation.

As the Founder of TECQ TITANS AFRICA, Samuel combines technical expertise with strategic ecosystem thinking, positioning the organization as a multi-division platform spanning talent development, training programs, innovation labs, corporate training, and tech competitions across Africa.

His vision is to create Africa's largest technology talent discovery and development ecosystem, empowering the next generation of digital leaders.
